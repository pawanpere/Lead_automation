## STAGE 2: WEIGHTED SCORING (1-5 scale)

If the lead passes all hard disqualifiers, score them on these 5 criteria:

### 1. Product Visual Appeal (Weight: 30%)
- Score 1: Plain/generic products, no lifestyle imagery, stock photos
- Score 3: Decent product photos, some lifestyle shots
- Score 5: Highly visual products, strong lifestyle branding, UGC-ready imagery

### 2. Brand Identity Strength (Weight: 20%)
- Score 1: Generic store, no brand story, looks like a dropship operation
- Score 3: Has a brand name and some identity but thin
- Score 5: Strong brand, recognizable aesthetic, community language, mission-driven

### 3. TikTok Presence Gap (Weight: 20%)
- Score 1: TikTok Shop link visible on site, active creator program evident (already doing it, skip)
- Score 3: TikTok link present but no Shop or low activity signals
- Score 5: No TikTok link anywhere on site despite strong brand (big opportunity)

### 4. Product Category Fit (Weight: 15%)
Use these tiers:
- **Tier A (score 5):** Fashion & Apparel, Beauty & Skincare, Health & Wellness, Fitness
- **Tier B (score 3-4):** Home & Kitchen, Pet Products, Food & Beverage, Baby & Kids, Books
- **Tier C (score 1-2):** Tech Accessories, Outdoor, Stationery, other niche categories
- **Poor fit (score 1):** Industrial, hardware, commodities, B2B

### 5. Estimated Scale (Weight: 15%)
- Score 1: Very early stage, fewer than 10 products, no social proof visible
- Score 3: Established store, moderate catalog, some reviews on site
- Score 5: Large catalog, visible reviews/testimonials, press logos, likely $1M+ revenue

## COMPOSITE SCORE CALCULATION
composite_score = (visual_appeal * 0.30) + (brand_identity * 0.20) + (tiktok_gap * 0.20) + (category_fit * 0.15) + (estimated_scale * 0.15)

## QUALIFICATION TIERS
- 4.0 - 5.0 = "hot" (qualified, pass to outreach immediately)
- 3.0 - 3.9 = "warm" (qualified, lower priority)
- 2.0 - 2.9 = "parked" (not qualified now, hold for future)
- Below 2.0 = "disqualified" (discard)

Minimum threshold for outreach: 3.0
