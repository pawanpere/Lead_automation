# Follow-Up Playbook — TikTok Shop x Ecommerce Brands

Full cadence, angle rotation, and breakup templates for ecommerce brand outreach across all categories.

---

## The Rule
Every follow-up must add something new. If your email is just "checking in" — delete it and write something with a new angle.

---

## Full 6-Email Cadence

| Email | Day | Gap | Angle | Purpose |
|-------|-----|-----|-------|---------|
| 1 | Day 1 | — | Brand love + TikTok Shop pitch | Establish relevance, show you know their brand |
| 2 | Day 4 | +3 | Creator economy proof | Different angle: how creators drive sales |
| 3 | Day 9 | +5 | Category insight / trend data | Give value: what's happening in their category |
| 4 | Day 16 | +7 | Direct question | Cut through: is this even a priority? |
| 5 | Day 25 | +9 | Referral ask | Am I reaching the right person? |
| 6 | Day 35 | +10 | Breakup | Close the loop, leave door open |

---

## Email-by-Email Guide

### Email 1: First Touch (Day 1)
Covered in `frameworks.md`. Use BOPA framework by default.

---

### Email 2: Creator Economy Proof (Day 4)

Shift the angle from "your brand is great" to "here's what's actually happening with similar brands on TikTok Shop."

**Example (adapt proof and content format to their category):**

**Subject:** quick follow up - [Brand]

> Hey [Name],
>
> Wanted to share something relevant — one of the ecommerce brands we work with just crossed 500 daily orders purely from creator-led TikTok Shop content. No paid ads, no influencer fees. Just product samples going to creators who genuinely wanted to try them.
>
> The reason it works so well for [their category]: [category-specific content hook — e.g., "people actually want to watch someone try on your pieces and style them" for fashion, or "unboxing and review content practically makes itself" for gadgets].
>
> Thought of [Brand] immediately because your products are exactly the kind creators love showing off.
>
> Worth a quick 15-min chat?
>
> [Name]

---

### Email 3: Category Insight (Day 9)

Give them something useful — a trend, a data point, an observation about their specific category on TikTok.

**Example:**

**Subject:** clean beauty on tiktok shop

> Hey [Name],
>
> Thought you'd find this interesting — [their category] is currently one of the fastest-growing categories on TikTok Shop. The brands getting in now are basically building their creator network before it gets crowded.
>
> What's wild is that most of these brands aren't spending on ads. The creator content itself is the growth engine — and once you have 500+ creators posting about your products, the compound effect is massive.
>
> Happy to share a quick breakdown of what's working in your category right now. No pitch — just useful context if TikTok Shop is anywhere on your roadmap.
>
> [Name]

---

### Email 4: Direct Question (Day 16)

Short. Honest. No fluff.

**Example:**

**Subject:** [Brand] + tiktok

> Hey [Name],
>
> Honest question — is TikTok Shop something you're actively thinking about, or is the timing just not right?
>
> No pressure either way. Just want to make sure I'm not filling your inbox with something that's not relevant to where you're at right now.
>
> [Name]

---

### Email 5: Referral Ask (Day 25)

**Example:**

**Subject:** right person?

> Hey [Name],
>
> I might be reaching out to the wrong person here — if someone else at [Brand] handles growth, e-commerce, or new sales channels, I'd love an intro.
>
> And if it is you — totally get it if the timing isn't right. No hard feelings.
>
> [Name]

---

### Email 6: Breakup (Day 35)

**Example:**

**Subject:** last one from me

> Hey [Name],
>
> I'll stop filling your inbox after this one.
>
> If TikTok Shop ever becomes a priority for [Brand], just reply here and I'll pick it right back up. The opportunity isn't going anywhere — but the early-mover advantage in your category won't last forever.
>
> Either way, love what you're building with [Brand]. Rooting for you.
>
> [Name]
>
> P.S. ~ if there's someone else at [Brand] I should be talking to, a name would be super helpful.

## Category-Specific Angles

**Universal angles (work for any ecommerce brand):**

| Angle | Why It Works | Example Hook |
|-------|-------------|-------------|
| Products are sold out | Demand signal = TikTok Shop is perfect timing | "Saw [product] is sold out — that demand is exactly what crushes on TikTok Shop" |
| They're already on TikTok | They understand the platform, just missing the shop | "You're already creating great content — Shop just adds the checkout layer" |
| New product launch | Fresh products = peak creator interest | "New launches get 2-3x the creator pickup rate" |
| Small/indie brand | Underdogs win on TikTok | "The brands winning on TikTok Shop aren't the big guys — it's brands exactly your size" |
| Seasonal moment | Holiday, summer, back-to-school | "Q4 creator content starts performing in September" |
| Competitor on TikTok Shop | FOMO + proof the category works | "Your competitors are already on TikTok Shop — [competitor] is doing [X]" |

**Category-specific content hooks:**

| Category | What Creator Content Looks Like |
|----------|-------------------------------|
| Beauty/Skincare | Routines, application, texture close-ups, before/after |
| Fashion/Apparel | Try-on hauls, outfit styling, "what I wore this week" |
| Supplements/Wellness | Morning routine, "what I take daily", health journeys |
| Home/Kitchen | Unboxing, before/after, "TikTok made me buy it", organization |
| Fitness/Sports | Workout demos, gym content, product in action |
| Food & Beverage | Taste tests, recipes, "try this with me", mukbang |
| Pet Products | Pet reactions, daily routines — highest organic engagement |
| Electronics/Gadgets | Reviews, demos, "things you didn't know you needed" |
| Kids/Baby | Parent routines, product hacks, "mom/dad favorites" |
| Jewelry/Accessories | Styling, close-up details, "everyday jewelry" content |

## What Never to Send
- "Just following up"
- "Did you see my last email?"
- "I wanted to make sure this didn't get lost"
- "I know you're busy but..."
- A forwarded copy of the original email
- Back-to-back emails same day
- Feature dump of everything you offer
- Generic "hope all is well"
